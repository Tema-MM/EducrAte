About Hoërskool Noordheuwel

Excellence in Education Since 2010

Our Story

Hoërskool Noordheuwel was established in 2010 with a vision to provide exceptional education that nurtures both academic excellence and personal growth. Located in the heart of Noordheuwel, our school has become a beacon of quality education in the community.

Over the past 15 years, we have built a reputation for academic excellence, with a 100% matric pass rate and numerous students excelling in various fields. Our commitment to holistic education ensures that students not only achieve academic success but also develop strong character, leadership skills, and a sense of community responsibility.
